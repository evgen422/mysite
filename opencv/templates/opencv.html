YOLOv8 + Deep_Sort. Car counting

This project shows the capabilities of YOLO and Deep Sort in objects detection and tracking. First we use YOLO v8 trained on COCO dataset to detect cars and then we feed the bounding boxes to Deep Sort. Cars moving to the right are in blue boxes, to the left are green. As soon as a car touches the edge of the screen, it is counted and turns white. Below the video, you can find traffic data analisys for 24 hours of counting.
